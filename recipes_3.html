Whole-wheat Bread

Yield: 16 Servings | Cooking Time: 2 hours

Ingredients









Directions

1. Whisk water, milk, and yeast together
2. Slowly add in the flour until just incorporated
3. Cover and let rise until double in size
4. Add remaining ingredients
5. Knead dough until it bounces back
6. Grease a pan and place dough inside
7. Cover and let rise until double in size (1st rise)
8. Punch down the dough and roll it out before putting it back
9. Cover and let rise until an inch above the rim
10. Preheat oven to 350F (177C)
11. Bake for 40 minutes or until golden brown
12. Let it cool before slicing and enjoy!
